About Us

Music Churn Predictor is a cutting-edge application designed to help music streaming services predict user churn. Our mission is to leverage machine learning and data analytics to provide valuable insights that improve user retention and business strategies.

Founded in 2023, our team consists of data scientists, developers, and music industry experts. We are passionate about helping businesses in the music streaming industry stay competitive by providing accurate, data-driven insights.

Our platform has been trusted by multiple music services to optimize their user engagement strategies and reduce churn rates. We are continuously evolving and improving to deliver better results to our users.
